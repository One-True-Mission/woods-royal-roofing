Our Story

The Roofer You Can Trust

Woods Royal Roofing LLC & Fencing is proudly woman owned and committed to excellence. We are based in Austin and serve homeowners and property owners across Round Rock, Georgetown, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Hutto, and the surrounding areas.

The roofing industry has a reputation problem: pushy salesmen, inflated quotes, and crews that disappear after the check clears. We built Woods Royal to be the opposite. Free inspections. Honest pricing. Quality workmanship. Then we put our name on it.

That is why our neighbors on Facebook recommend us 100% of the time. Every project starts with an inspection and ends with a beautiful new roof, and we treat everything in between like our reputation depends on it. Because it does.
