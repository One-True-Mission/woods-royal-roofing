Privacy Policy

Last updated July 1, 2026

Woods Royal Roofing LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us. When you submit our inspection request form, that includes your name and phone number, and, if you provide them, your email address, property address, and any notes about your roof, fence, or project. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your request, schedule and perform your inspection, prepare an estimate, assist with any insurance claim, and communicate with you about your project. We may keep a record of our communications for our own business purposes.

How Your Information Is Handled

Our website is hosted as static pages, and our inspection form is processed by a third-party form provider that delivers your message to us by email. That provider processes your submission solely to deliver it. We do not use advertising trackers or sell data collected through this site.

Your Privacy Rights (Texas Residents)

Under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA), Texas residents have the right to confirm whether we process their personal data, to access and correct that data, to request deletion of data they have provided, and to obtain a copy of it. You also have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. To make a request, contact us using the phone or email below and we will respond as required by law.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to serve you and to meet our ordinary business and legal needs, after which we dispose of it.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for adults seeking roofing and fencing services. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or your information, contact Woods Royal Roofing LLC at 832.413.7622 or info.woodsroyalroofingllc@gmail.com, Austin, TX.
